AxiomCoders PDF Reports .NET Component is .NET assembly that is used for generating PDF reports from any kind of data.

By using this component you will give your applications ability to generate PDF reports.

Some examples on implementation of this component would be:

- Your application can make price-lists in PDF formats where you keep your products and data in SQL Server.
- Your application can make nicely designed catalogs of your products with images and descriptions where you keep your data in database.
- Your application need to generate reports for data that is gathered through some network or maybe some hardware (in this case you would use manual data feeding mechanism by callbacks)

Main Advantages of PDF Reports .NET Component:

- You don't need to have all data ready in your memory to generate your report. If you for example have 1,000,000 rows in table that you want to generate you can do this by manually feeding PDF Reports with blocks of data.
- Your data does not need to be available at all once, and you can feed PDF Reports with data as data arrives
- If relationship between your data is really complicated and require some manual intervention PDF Reprots can handle this
- Data used for generating reports can come from any possible data source. (e.g. you could use information from hardware ports in order to give data to your PDF Reports generator)
- You can generate your PDF file continuosly for as long as you want. This is useful in cases when you receive your data with some delay. (e.g. You get your data minute by minute from your network).
- You have more freedom on how your reports will look like. Please check our online samples page to see what kind of reports can be created here.
