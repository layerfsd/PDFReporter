Now we are going to make some relationships between data streams and their columns. This step is not required and you can skip it if you don't need any relationship. This kind of relationship is same as relationship thing in SQL Servers.

This is done by selecting data stream and column on the left and on the right and press button "Set Relationship ->"

Off course if you made a mistake, you can simply delete relationship by selecting it and pressing button "Delete".
This is left to right relationship meaning one-to-many relationship from left to right.

Example:

If you have Persons data stream on the left side and Telephone data stream on the right side, you would connect ID on the left with PersonID on the right, making situation where one person can have multiple telephones.

After you done press button "Next >>"
